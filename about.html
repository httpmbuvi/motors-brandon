About Brandon Motors

Kenya's most trusted car dealership.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for cars and a commitment to service, Brandon Motors has grown from a small startup into one of Kenya's most reputable car dealerships. We are a Kenyan-owned business dedicated to helping our fellow citizens achieve the dream of car ownership. Our experience in sourcing, importing, and selling high-quality new and used vehicles ensures that every customer drives away with confidence and a smile.

Our Mission

To make car ownership affordable, transparent, and accessible for every Kenyan.

Our Vision

To be Kenya’s most trusted car dealership, known for quality, integrity, and exceptional customer service.
